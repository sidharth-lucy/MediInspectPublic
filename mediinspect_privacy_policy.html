Privacy Policy – MediInspect

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

MediInspect respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the MediInspect mobile application.

MediInspect is designed to provide general educational information about medicines and health topics. The app does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or prescriptions.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our services, MediInspect may collect limited information.

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Basic authentication information

Technical Information

The app may automatically collect certain non-personal information, such as:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App performance data
  • Crash reports
  • Anonymous usage statistics

Health or Medical Data

MediInspect does not intentionally collect personal medical records or sensitive health data. Any symptoms or health topics entered in the app are processed only to provide informational results and are not used for medical diagnosis.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Manage user accounts
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse

3. Data Sharing

MediInspect does not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Limited data may be processed by trusted third-party services used to operate the app, such as authentication services, crash reporting, and analytics tools.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Use

MediInspect is intended for general audiences. Children under the age of 13 should only use the app under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

6. Educational Purpose Only

The content provided in MediInspect is for educational and informational purposes only. The information presented should not be interpreted as medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

7. Medical Disclaimer

MediInspect does not provide medical consultation, diagnosis, or prescriptions. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please contact a qualified healthcare provider or local emergency services immediately.

9. User Responsibility

Users should not rely solely on the app for medical decisions and should always consult with qualified health professionals.
